PUPPY

FEATURING FREE RUN CHICKEN & TURKEY, WILD CAUGHT FISH AND NEST LAID EGGS

PUPPIES OF ALL BREEDS EVOLVED AS CARNIVORES, POSSESSING A BIOLOGICAL NEED FOR A DIET RICH AND VARIED IN FRESH WHOLE MEATS, WITH SMALLER AMOUNTS OF WHOLE FRUITS, VEGETABLES AND GRASSES.

That’s why BARKA delivers a symphony of cage-free chicken and turkey, nest-laid eggs, and wild-caught walleye and herring — all raised or fished within our region and delivered to our kitchens FRESH EACH DAY, so they’re brimming with goodness to perfectly nourish the growth of puppies.

Loaded with the richly-nourishing proteins and fats that all puppies require for peak health, BARKA’s award-winning low-glycemic puppy food supports lean muscle mass while supplying all of the energy your puppy needs to take on his exciting world.

Prepared from Canada’s best and freshest ingredients in our award-winning kitchens, BARKA is guaranteed to keep your puppy happy, healthy and strong.
